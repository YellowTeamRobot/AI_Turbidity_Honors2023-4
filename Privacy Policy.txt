Turbidivision Privacy Policy
-------------------------

The Turbidivision app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It functions entirely on your device and does not connect to the internet or transmit any information to external servers.

No user data is collected, used, or shared by the app developer.
However, please note that your device's operating system or app store (namely Google Play Services https://policies.google.com/privacy) may collect data as part of their normal operation. This is outside the control of the app and is governed by their respective privacy policies.
